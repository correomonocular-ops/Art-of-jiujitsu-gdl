Meet Our Instructors

Rafael Mendes

6x World Champion, Co-Founder of AOJ

Rafael Mendes is a six-time IBJJF World Champion and one of the most dominant featherweights in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu history.

He is widely respected for his innovative guard work, dynamic movement, and technical precision, revolutionizing modern Jiu Jitsu with his style.

Alongside his brother Guilherme Mendes, he co-founded Art of Jiu Jitsu (AOJ) in Costa Mesa, California, in 2012.

After retiring from competition, Rafael dedicated himself fully to coaching and building a new generation of elite athletes through AOJ’s world-class training program.
